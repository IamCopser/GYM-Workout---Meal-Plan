  • No junk, sugar, or outside food: One bite ruins the deficit
  • Hydrate: 4–5L water daily (especially for creatine)
  • Timing matters:

    • Post-workout = whey + creatine (within 20 mins)

    • Dinner = light, protein-only (no roti/rice)
